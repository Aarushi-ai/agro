About Agrocare

Rooted in soil. Driven by farmers.

Incepted in the year 2011, we are a group of cohort individuals with diverse backgrounds promoting AgroCare. Our company, a small-scale business entity, is involved in cultivation as well as supply/export of seaweeds and organic bio-stimulants. Legally a partnership firm, we adhere to high business principles in every venture. Quality is the primary attribute imbibed in our products, leaving no scope for complaints. Presently serving clientele across India, we look forward to fruitful business opportunities abroad. We also supply customised products according to customer requirements.

PULKIT JAIN — Founder, AgroCare

The Story Behind the Soil

In 2011, Pulkit Jain had one question that refused to leave him: why do India's hardest-working people — our farmers — remain the poorest? Armed with research, curiosity, and deep respect for the land, he began exploring nature's own answers. Seaweeds, humic acids, bio-stimulants — the ocean and earth held solutions that no chemical factory could match. Starting small from the coast of Gujarat, AgroCare was born — not as a business, but as a mission. A mission to reduce what farmers spend, increase what their fields yield, and restore the dignity that every farmer deserves. Today, 8+ years and 18+ districts later, that mission continues — one farm, one farmer, one season at a time.

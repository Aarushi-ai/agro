About Agrocare

Rooted in soil. Driven by farmers.

Incepted in the year 2011, Agrocare is a partnership firm driven by a group of cohort individuals with diverse backgrounds, united by a single vision — to transform Indian agriculture through certified organic inputs. We are involved in the cultivation, supply, and export of seaweeds and organic bio-stimulants.

Guided by the inspiration of our founder Vardabhai, we set out to research and develop products that could improve fields naturally, reduce dependency on harmful chemicals, and lift the financial condition of farmers from poverty — helping them spend less, earn more, and live better lives while keeping their land fertile for generations.

What began as a small company with a big dream has grown into a trusted name across 18+ districts of India. Quality is the primary attribute imbibed in every product we make, leaving no scope for complaints from our customers. We currently serve clients across India and are actively looking forward to fruitful business opportunities abroad. We also supply customized products according to customer requirements and specifications.

Founder & Visionary

Vardabhai

With roots in Gujarat's farming communities, Vardabhai envisioned a future where every Indian farmer could access certified organic inputs at affordable prices — and Agrocare was born from that dream.
